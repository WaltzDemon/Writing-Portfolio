Inventory System Designed for Small to Mid Sized Non-Profit Donation Upsell Store

The most cost-effective solution was a subscription-based Online Inventory Management Software to track the donation and selling of non-expiring product. The Online Inventory Management Software, now referred to as "Clearly Inventory" had to be set up for Non-Profit use.
